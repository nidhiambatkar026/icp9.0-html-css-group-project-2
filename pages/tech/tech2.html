Pixel 9 Pro and Pixel 9 Pro XL launched.

Date: Aug 16,2024

Google Pixel 9 Pro XL has a 6.8-inch Super Actua display with a peak brightness of 3,000 nits and 1344 x 2992 pixels resolution. It is an LTPO OLED panel with 486 ppi, 20:9 aspect ratio and 1Hz-120Hz refresh rate. The smartphone is powered by Google Tensor G4 SoC paired with a Titan M2 security coprocessor, 16GB RAM and up to 512GB storage. On the camera front, Google Pixel 9 Pro XL comes with a 50MP Octa PD wide camera, a 48MP Quad PD ultrawide shooter and a 48MP Quad PD telephoto camera. There is a 42MP Dual PD selfie camera on the front. The device houses a 5,060mAh battery and comes with IP68 dust and water resistance rating. Google Pixel 9 is equipped with a 6.3-inch Actua display with a peak brightness of 2,700 nits. The full-HD (1080 x 2424 pixels at 422 PPI) OLED display has 20:9 aspect ratio and 60-120Hz refresh rate. Under the hood is Google Tensor G4 SoC that is specially designed to handle AI and machine learning tasks. The chipset is coupled with a Titan M2 security coprocessor, 12GB RAM and up to 256GB storage. As far as the camera is concerned, the phone gets a 50MP Octa PD wide camera with ƒ/1.68 aperture and 82-degree field of view. There is an ultrawide camera that packs a 48MP Quad PD sensor with an ultrawide lens that has ƒ/1.7 aperture and 123-degree field of view. For selfies, the handset features a 10.5MP Dual PD selfie camera. The device is backed by a 4,700mAh battery and comes with IP68 dust and water resistance rating.
