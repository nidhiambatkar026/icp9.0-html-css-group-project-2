Taylor Swift fans flock to London for final leg of Eras Tour after Vienna terrorism plot

Date : Thus , Aug 15

Taylor Swift has resumed her "Eras Tour" in London after canceling three shows last week due to a foiled plan for a terrorist attack at her concert. Swift kicked off a run of five shows at Wembley Stadium on Aug. 15 when doors officially opened at 3:30 p.m. London time. The show started at 4:55 p.m., or 11:55 a.m. ET, with openers Sofia Isella and Paramore.Swift was scheduled to perform at Ernst-Happel-Stadion Aug. 8, 9 and 10 for her second to last stop on the European leg of the tour. Swift confirmed the cancellations via her website. The news was originally announced by Barracuda Music, a live music promotion company in Austria. Just over a week ago, three of her "Eras Tour" shows in Vienna, Austria, were canceled Aug. 7.
“With confirmation from government officials of a planned terrorist attack at Ernst Happel Stadium, we have no choice but to cancel the three scheduled shows for everyone’s safety,” Barracuda Music said in a statement. “All tickets will be automatically refunded within the next 10 business days.” Austrian police arrested two men Aug. 7 who allegedly had detailed plans for carrying out attacks on major Vienna events, including Swift's concerts. “The main perpetrator confessed that he wanted to carry out a suicide attack with two accomplices. He obtained explosives and weapons," Austrian Chancellor Karl Nehammer said on X Aug. 8.
