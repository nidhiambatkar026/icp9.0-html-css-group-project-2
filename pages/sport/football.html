Gurpreet Singh Sandhu's brilliant save helps India win SAFF Championship for a record 9th time.

Date: JUL 05,2023




India emerged victorious in the SAFF Championship by defeating Kuwait in a thrilling penalty shoot-out. The match was evenly contested, ending with a 1-1 draw at the conclusion of regular play, leading to a penalty shoot-out. After five rounds of penalties, the scoreline remained tied at 4-4, forcing the match into sudden death. It was during this crucial moment that Gurpreet Singh Sandhu, India's goalkeeper, showcased his exceptional skills by making an incredible save. Meanwhile, Mahesh Naorem successfully scored for India, while Gurpreet's save denied Kuwait a goal. This triumph marks India's second victory in a penalty shoot-out and their record-breaking ninth SAFF Championship title.
