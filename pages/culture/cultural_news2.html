Pandit Tanmoy Bose honored with Sri Chinmoy Culture-Light Award

Chinmoy Aspiration-Ground in Queens, New York, honoured tabla exponent Pandit Tanmoy Bose, with the prestigious Sri Chinmoy Culture-Light Award. This award is given to individuals with an extraordinary ability to illuminate humanity through their exceptional contributions in various cultural fields, including music, literature, visual arts, diplomacy, academics, and sports Pandit Bose has played tabla around the globe.I am deeply humbled and honoured to have received the Sri Chinmoy Culture-Light Award. Sri Chinmoys teachings of peace and harmony resonate deeply with my ethos, and I am grateful for this recognition of my efforts to bring the ancient spirit of music to audiences worldwide.
